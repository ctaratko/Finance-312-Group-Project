Pfizer Overview

Pfizer [PFE] is a Pharmaceutical & Biotechnology Corporation. Headquartered in New York, it’s one of the largest research based drug manufacturers. For the reason of choosing Pfizer, it was due to it’s media activity within the past 6 years, how it was one of the leading names through COVID-19, and it’s heavy growth of R&D Projects that base themselves in Influenza Vaccines, Oncology, and Obesity/Metabolic Health. As of recent, their key business situated around COVID-19 Vaccinations and similar products, but with the decreasing demand for such products, they’ve begun pushing increased efforts into their Metabolic & Oncological research.

For Pfizer’s Senior Leadership, specifically their CEO, Dr. Albert Bourla has been regarded as a great leader for the Pfizer company by many outlets, although his performance also seems to be politically based to an extent. Gaining the title of CNN Business CEO of the year in 2021, he has also led the stock price of Pfizer from 2021 to present date [~2026] in a decreasing manner from ~$40 to ~$30 (-25%). This can primarily be related to the significant market changes of COVID-19 no longer being a large market segment, and Pfizer being too heavily invested into it.

As for Pfizer’s Turnover Rate of Employees and Management. The Employee Turnover Rate compares equivocally to other comparable Pharmaceutical Giants, while it’s Management Turnover Rate is considered to be higher than the average Pharmaceutical Firm.

Quantitative Analysis – Data Manipulation

Daily Historical Prices for the Stock [2024]:

Pfizer$Date <- as.Date(Pfizer$Date, format = "%d-%b-%y")


DailyStockPrice <- Pfizer %>%
  filter(year(Date) == 2024) %>% 
  mutate(DailyReturn = round((`Close*` / `Close*`[-1]) - 1, digits = 5))

values <- DailyStockPrice %>% 
  select(DailyReturn) %>% 
  summarise(
    m_DR = round(mean(DailyReturn), digits = 6),
    sd_DR = round(sd(DailyReturn), digits = 6)
  )
values <- as.data.frame(values)
values
      m_DR    sd_DR
1 0.000151 0.016617
ggplot(DailyStockPrice, aes(
  x = Date,
  y = `Close*`
)) +
  geom_line(aes(y = `Close*`),size = 1, show.legend = F) +
  geom_line(aes(y = High, color = "lightgreen"),size = .25, show.legend = F) +
  geom_line(aes(y = Low, color = "red4"),size = .25, show.legend = F) +
  theme_solarized() + 
  labs(
    title = "Pfizer Closing Values [2024]"
  )
